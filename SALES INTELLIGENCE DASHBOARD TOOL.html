Executive Revenue Intelligence Report
Generated For CEO & CFO · Based On Q2 2026 CRM Dataset
May 16, 2026 · 09:00 PKT
01 · Executive Summary
Revenue momentum is positive but uneven. Gross revenue reached $8.4M in Q2, up 14.2% QoQ, driven primarily by enterprise deal expansion in North America. Overall quota attainment sits at 87% — 13 points below target — signaling execution gaps in LATAM and APAC. Pipeline velocity improved to $142K/day. Average deal size surged 21% to $48K, validating the upmarket strategy.
02 · Underperforming Areas
  • LATAM Territory: 61% quota attainment. 38-day longer sales cycle vs North America. Insufficient local SE coverage.
  • SMB Segment: Win rate dropped from 44% to 29% QoQ. Trial-to-paid conversion churn 22% above benchmark.
  • PS Attach Rate: Only 18% of new enterprise logos buying PS bundles vs 40% target. ~$1.1M lost NRR.
03 · Win-Loss Diagnostics
  • Proposal → Negotiation (41% stall): Competitor discounting 18–25% forcing unplanned concessions with no structured approval workflow.
  • Discovery → Proposal (28% stall): APAC reps advancing underprepared opportunities. 6-day avg vs 14-day benchmark.
  • Closed-Lost (34% "no decision"): Insufficient executive sponsorship and weak business case documentation at economic buyer level.
04 · Strategic Action Plan
01
Deploy Competitive Pricing Playbook

Issue structured discount approval matrix. Cap field discounting at 12% without VP sign-off. Bundle PS attach incentive ($2K SPiF per qualifying deal).

02
LATAM Emergency Coverage Rebalancing

Reassign 2 senior NA SEs to LATAM accounts above $50K ACV for 60 days. Activate deal coaching alerts for any LATAM deal stagnant >21 days.

03
Executive Sponsor Program

Mandate CRO/VP-level sponsor mapping for all opportunities >$75K ACV in Stage 3+. Launch Business Value Assessment template targeting 15% reduction in no-decision outcomes.

Five metrics that form the minimum viable measurement layer for any B2B SaaS sales operation — each with its exact calculation method, update cadence, and impact tier.

Daily Activity Rate
(Calls + Emails + Demos) ÷ Working Days
Tracks raw seller productivity inputs. Early warning signal before pipeline quality deteriorates.
Cadence: Daily

Quota Attainment %
Closed Revenue ÷ Quota × 100
Most-watched number in any sales org. Drives comp, territory, and coaching prioritization.
Cadence: Weekly
■ High Impact
⚡
Pipeline Velocity
(Deals × Win Rate × ACV) ÷ Cycle Days
Composite metric synthesizing volume, conversion, and deal value into a dollar-per-day throughput.
Cadence: Weekly
■ High Impact
🔄
Stage Conversion Rate
Deals Advancing ÷ Deals Entering × 100
Identifies where deals stall. Drop at Proposal = pricing. Drop at Discovery = qualification problems.
Cadence: Weekly
■ High Impact
⏱
Average Sales Cycle
Sum Days to Close ÷ Closed Deals
Determines forecast reliability and resource planning accuracy. Exposes outliers rushing or stalling.
Cadence: Monthly
■ Medium Impact
Each KPI's optimal chart type — chosen for data shape and the question a manager needs to answer in under 3 seconds of glance time.
KPI 01 · Activity Rate
Grouped Bar Chart
Compares calls vs emails vs demos per rep side-by-side, revealing activity mix imbalances that impact conversion.
X: Rep · Y: Count · Color: Type
KPI 02 · Quota Attainment
Bullet / Gauge Chart
Shows current attainment vs target and benchmark — three data points in one compact strip readable instantly.
Fill: Actual % · Line: Target · Band: Prior
KPI 03 · Pipeline Velocity
Smooth Area Line
Area charts communicate trajectory and momentum better than bars. Rate-of-change is intuitively visible.
X: Week · Y: $/Day · Dashed: Forecast
KPI 04 · Stage Conversion
Funnel Chart
Makes drop-off between stages immediately visible. Managers see which stage is the biggest conversion leak.
Stages: Prospect → Close
KPI 05 · Sales Cycle
Box Plot / Scatter
Shows median, spread, and outliers at once. Reveals reps with wildly inconsistent cycle times — the real coaching insight.
X: Rep · Y: Days · Dot: Outlier
